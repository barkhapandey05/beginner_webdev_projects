APJ Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Missile Man Of India

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, popularly known as APJ Abdul Kalam, was an Indian scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India. Kalam was a key figure in India's civilian space program and military missile development efforts, earning him the nickname "MissileMan ofIndia". He played a crucial role in the development of India's first satellite launch vehicle and ballistic missile technology. Kalam was also known for his dedication to education and youth empowerment, inspiring millions with his vision for a developed India. He passed away on July 27, 2015, while delivering a lecture at the Indian Institute of Management Shillong. His legacy continues to inspire generations of Indians and people around the world.
